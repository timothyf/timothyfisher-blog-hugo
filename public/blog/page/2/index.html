My Journey to Quikly

April 22, 2016

3 minute read

Within the last month I left a large established Detroit-based services company to join a small technology startup in downtown Detroit. The company I joined was Quikly. Quikly is a platform that helps marketers drive immediate and measurable results by digitally engaging consumers in a friendly competition to earn rewards. The platform incorporates a lot of pretty cool technologies which I plan on talking about over time. Some of Quikly’s customers include: Victoria’s Secret, Dominos, Microsoft, Toys R Us, Quicken Loans, Pet Supplies Plus and Kraft.
